Privacy Policy for Kaomoji Drawer

Effective Date: September 7, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kaomoji Drawer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This policy outlines our data handling practices.

2. Information We Collect

Kaomoji Drawer is designed with your privacy in mind and collects minimal personal information:

  • Custom Kaomojis: Any custom kaomojis you create within the app.
  • Recently Used Kaomojis: A list of your recently used kaomojis for quick access.
  • AI-Generated Kaomojis: If you use our AI generation feature, we temporarily process your prompts to generate kaomojis.

We do not collect any personal identifiable information, location data, or usage statistics.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Kaomoji Drawer service.
  • To improve and personalize your experience within the app.

4. Data Storage

All data (custom kaomojis and recently used kaomojis) is stored locally on your device. We do not store this information on our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Kaomoji Drawer uses a third-party API for AI-generated kaomojis. When you use this feature, your prompts are sent to our AI service provider. We do not share any other information with this provider, and prompts are not stored after processing.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your recently used kaomojis within the app at any time.
  • Delete any custom kaomojis you've created.

8. Children's Privacy

Kaomoji Drawer is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
